What can I say? I love technology and I love music. I'm obsessed with both and I don't care who knows it. I'm a Software Engineer based in Austin, and my experience in both the Music and Technology industries have been paramount to my personal and professional development.

I consider myself lucky to have grown up congruent to the technology industry, and to have experienced the evolution of modern computers first-hand. Learning the DOS commands to navigate around our first home computer as a child, teaching myself HTML and building my first websites as a teenager, and my more recent studies in app/web development have all been the catalysts for my computer obsession. I've never lost touch with my technological curiosity and I always strive to be learning new programming languages and concepts.

As a younger adult, I also ventured into the world of Music Technology with studies in Music Production and Recording Engineering. I've ran my own successful DJ business on the side for the past 15+ years, and continue to do so on the weekends to help keep my creative flames lit. Music is a true common denominator for bringing people together, and I thrive on the human connection it provides for us all.

The combination of these experiences and passions inevitably led me to software development. I truly love working with people and bringing minds together to create wonderful and powerful things, and I personally believe that science and technology are absolutely essential to the greater good of the world.
