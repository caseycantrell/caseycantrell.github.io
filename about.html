About Me

While programming certainly is my main focus, I also try to find the time to enjoy leisurely activities. You can find me out on the water of Downtown Austin in my kayak, or taking my dog on an off-leash adventure on the trails. I also enjoy skateboarding, collecting rare sneakers, going to concerts, and I'm always looking for good Thai food recommendations.

I recently relocated from Kansas City, MO to Austin, TX in 2018 and have grown very fond of the city. From the myriad outdoor activities to the bustling music scene, it's hard not to get attached to a special place like this.

Outside of the technology world, I also have spent many years in the music industry. I studied Recording Engineering and used that knowledge to produce my own music, which I was then able to monetize. I've also been highly active as a DJ, first in the Kansas City, MO area then in Austin, and continue to do that most weekends.

I also have a dog named Quincy. He's a boxer/labrador mix and he is literally the sweetest dog in the charted universe.

Thanks for stopping by. Please feel free to reach out and I'll get back to you post haste.
